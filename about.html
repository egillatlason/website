About

I write goofy stories

A few years ago I had the momentously stupid idea to write literature. Which hasn’t been lucrative, because literature usually isn't. Also, I haven't published anything. What it has afforded me was the chance to meet and better express myself to the people who enjoyed my work, even though I haven't written much or shown it to many people. I hope to find many more of you. I supposed I started writing because I wanted to do something meaningful and novels seemed like a good place to find meaning. But reading is boring, yo. And writing turned out to be difficult. So I’ve been writing short stories. You can read them here: Stories
